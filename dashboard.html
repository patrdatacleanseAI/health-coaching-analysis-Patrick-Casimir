Health Coaching Platform — Executive Dashboard

Executive Summary

  • Retention declines significantly from 30 to 90 days, indicating early disengagement risk.
  • High-risk members represent the majority of the population and the biggest ROI opportunity.
  • Clinical success is driven more by engagement behavior than baseline risk.
  • Behavioral signals (steps, nudges, coaching, app usage) strongly influence outcomes.

1. Cohort Retention

Retention drops sharply by 90 days, highlighting a critical early intervention window.

2. Risk Distribution

~67% of members are high-risk, representing the largest opportunity for impact.

3. Success by Risk Tier

Similar success rates across tiers show engagement is the main driver of outcomes.

4. Behavioral Drivers

Top drivers include steps, nudge response, coaching sessions, and app logins.

5. Engagement vs Outcome

Higher nudge responsiveness correlates with greater HbA1c reduction.

Key Insights

  • Most churn happens within first 30 days.
  • High-risk population is large but not harder to improve.
  • Engagement behaviors directly impact clinical outcomes.
  • Nudge response is a strong predictor of success.

3 Recommendations

  1. Day-21 intervention trigger to prevent early drop-off.
  2. Cap high-risk intervention cost at $300 to maintain ROI.
  3. Drive 7,000 steps/day as a key behavior change metric.
