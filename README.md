## Key Insights

- Retention declines significantly from 30 to 90 days, with early drop-off as the main challenge
- High-risk members represent ~67% of the population and the largest opportunity
- Engagement behaviors (steps, coaching, app usage, nudges) drive clinical outcomes
- Nudge responsiveness strongly correlates with HbA1c improvement

---

## Recommendations

1. Implement a **Day-21 intervention trigger** to prevent early disengagement  
2. Cap **High-Risk intervention cost at $300** to maintain ROI  
3. Promote **7,000 daily steps** as a key behavioral target
